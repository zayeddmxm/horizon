MOVING ON!
Toronto Toronto left an imprint on me in unexpected ways. It wasn’t just the skyline or the subway tunnels — it was the layered sense of identity, the quiet tension of navigating belonging in a city that prides itself on diversity. Walking through different neighborhoods felt like flipping through languages, colors, and smells, all stitched together by the rhythm of streetcars and winter wind. Toronto taught me how to hold multiple versions of myself at once — immigrant, artist, outsider, local — and move between them without explanation. It wasn’t always easy, but it was formative.
